About December 10, 2019


Hello!

I'm Deepti Mahesh, currently based in Hyderabad. I'm a third year Honors student pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Engineering and a research intern at Cognitive Science Labs (IIIT) under Professor Kavita Vemuri.

Currently my Honors project involves analysing and parsing through datasets involving eyetracking of drivers during daylight. I aim to create and apply models and generate observations based on perception and visual memory of a set of participants.

My passion lies in identifying patterns in the unknown and this remains one of many reasons for my interest in data science and analysis.
