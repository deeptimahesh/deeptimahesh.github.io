About Me

Hello!

I'm Deepti Mahesh, currently based in Colorado, having finished my master's studies at CU Boulder. I have 2+ years of work experience dealing with ML, CV and full-stack development.

My passion lies in computer vision and its vast applications, allowing a computer to aid humans such that they can tap into their best creative potential and imagination. I love exploring my creative side and seek to merge this with my Computer Science background. My main hobbies include digital art, world-building, learning random phrases in new languages and cheering on various cursed sports teams.

Explore my webpage and feel free to drop me an email at deepti.mahesh@colorado.edu
